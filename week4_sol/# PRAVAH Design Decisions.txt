# PRAVAH Design Decisions
Locked down at Week 4, Milestone 2. Changes require mentor approval.

## Pipeline
* Issue width: 2
* Fetch width: 2
* Commit width: 2

## Register file
* Architectural regs: 32 (RV32I)
* Physical regs: 48
* Free list: 16 entries

## Reservation stations
* ALU: 4 entries (this week's module)
* MUL: 2 entries (Week 6)
* LSU: 2 entries (Week 6)

## ROB
* Depth: 8
* Dispatch/commit width: 2

## Functional units
* 2 ALUs (1-cycle)
* 1 MUL (3-cycle pipelined)
* 1 LSU (1-cycle, blocking)

## Branch predictor
* 2-bit BHT, 256 entries, indexed by PC[9:2]
* No BTB (predict-taken pays 1 extra cycle for target computation)

## ISA subset (13 instructions)
* ADD, SUB, ADDI
* AND, OR, XOR, SLL, SRL, SLT
* LW, SW
* BEQ, BNE, JAL

## Memory
* IMem: 1 KB, $readmemh init
* DMem: 1 KB, single-port, combinational read

## Out of scope (DO NOT IMPLEMENT)
* Caches, LSQ, store forwarding, tournament predictor, FP, exceptions, 4-wide issue
